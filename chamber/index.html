Current Events

Federal Advocacy Visit 2024

The Chamber will be bringing a group of Austin business and community leaders to advocate for pro-business policies in Washington, DC from June 11th-13th. Full details and registration information will be available shortly.

Austin Gives Awards - May 7, 2024

Each year, we invite the business community to nominate businesses that do an outstanding job of giving back to our Central Texas community. Join us at the JW Marriott on May 7th to honor our 2024 winners.

2024 Infrastructure Summit - April 10, 2024

We are gathering on April 1st to discuss everything unfolding in transportation, transit, water, power, and more in the Greater Austin area! This event offers a unique chance for business and community leaders to gain insights into Austin's future directly from regional experts and elected officials.
